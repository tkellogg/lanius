# Claude

> The intent-and-spec model. "Gets Tim" better than the others.

## Planning (its home)

- Use Claude for planning (phases 1–2), ideally at **high/xhigh** effort. It is
  trusted for intent capture and spec work in a way GPT/GLM are not.
- Claude is one of only two models allowed to plan; the other is
  [fable.md](fable.md). See [role-planner.md](role-planner.md).

## Other roles

- Not the default implementer or verifier — bank it (and Fable) for planning;
  the cheaper tiers build and check ([role-implementer.md](role-implementer.md),
  [role-verifier.md](role-verifier.md)).

## The invariant

- **Planning never flexes**: never hand planning to GPT-5.5 or GLM-5.2, even when
  impl/verify models are swapped for credit reasons.
