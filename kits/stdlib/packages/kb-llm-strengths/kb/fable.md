# Fable

> Unparalleled at planning. Expensive — bank it.

## Planning (its home)

- Fable is the strongest planner available. Use it for planning (phases 1–2)
  when you can afford it; it is one of only two models allowed to plan (the
  other is [claude.md](claude.md)). See [role-planner.md](role-planner.md).
- Because it is expensive, bank it for planning — and occasionally for the
  hardest / most critical **verifications** ([role-verifier.md](role-verifier.md))
  when it is available.

## The invariant

- **Planning never flexes**: planning stays with Claude or Fable. GPT-5.5 and
  GLM-5.2 build and check, but never own the plan.
